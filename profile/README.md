# JLB Digital

## Overview

JLB Digital is the digital initiative of the Jeugd Luchtvaart Brigade (JLB).
Its purpose is to design and develop systems, tools, and frameworks that support the educational, operational, and developmental goals of the organization.

The focus is not limited to software alone, but extends to the broader digital ecosystem required to support cadet learning, instructor guidance, and organizational growth.

---

## Mission

To enable cadets of the JLB to develop strong foundations in aviation knowledge, leadership, and discipline through well-structured digital systems and tools.

---

## Vision

To create an integrated digital environment where:

* Aviation theory (aligned with PPL(A) concepts) is accessible and structured
* Learning progress is measurable and guided
* Instructors are supported with tools and insights
* Cadets develop both technical knowledge and leadership skills

---

## Scope

JLB Digital develops and maintains:

* Learning platforms
* Curriculum models and knowledge structures
* Assessment and tracking systems
* Instructor support tools
* Digital processes and workflows

---

## Structure of this Organization

This organization is structured into three main layers:

### 1. Strategy (Why)

Repositories that define:

* Organizational context
* Curriculum and learning objectives
* Needs and problem statements
* Solution vision

Example:

* `jlb-strategy`

---

### 2. Program (What)

Repositories that define:

* Roadmaps
* Projects and initiatives
* Tasks and deliverables

Example:

* `jlb-program`

---

### 3. Products (How)

Repositories that implement:

* Software systems
* Tools and platforms

Examples:

* `jlb-learning-platform`
* `jlb-assessment-engine`

---

## Development Approach

Development follows a structured flow:

1. Needs are identified at the strategic level
2. Problems are defined and analyzed
3. Solutions are proposed and structured
4. Programs and projects are created
5. Products are developed and iterated

---

## AI-Assisted Development

This organization leverages AI-assisted development (e.g. Claude Code) to:

* Translate structured knowledge into working systems
* Maintain alignment between strategy and implementation
* Improve development speed and consistency

To support this, repositories contain structured and versioned documentation that serves as context for AI systems.

---

## Principles

* Clarity over complexity
* Structure before implementation
* Traceability between idea and implementation
* Incremental development
* Alignment with JLB mission and values
